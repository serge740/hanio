About Honey

Over its history as a food, the main uses of honey are in
cooking, baking, desserts, as a spread on bread, as an addition
to various beverages such as tea, and as a sweetener in some
commercial beverages.

Due to its energy density, honey is an important food for
virtually all hunter-gatherer cultures in warm climates, with the
Hadza people ranking honey as their favorite food.
